Picture Your Selfie at Metrotown!




Got selfie? We want to see your best look!

From March 10 - 22, 2015, stop by Grand Court and check out our massive interactive “Selfie Wall”. The Wall, which measures 50 feet by 19 feet, houses five 70-inch LCD screens that live stream moderated customer photos uploaded to Twitter or Instagram using the hashtag #myselfiemystyle. Customers who upload a photo will also be entered for a chance to win daily gift card prizes and a grand prize $1000 shopping spree. Click here for FAQ.

In keeping with the digital theme, starting March 6th, 2015, we will also launch “Picture Your Selfie" - a mobile app where customers can upload their best selfie, decorate the photo with frames and stickers, and then share the image on their social media channels with the hashtag #pictureyourselfie. All uploaded photos on Twitter will be eligible for a chance to win a $25 gift card daily prize, or one of the $500 Grand Prize or Judge's Choice Prize. Picture Your Selfie can be accessed at selfie.metropoliscontests.com. Contest ends March 27, 2015.
